Notes

From either stubbornness or incompetence, I'm hopeless with lectures, so I have a high appreciation for clear books. In particular I'm grateful for Kenneth Kuttler's notes on analysis. The fields aside from maths are less fortunate in their undergrad books. "Introductory" should not mean "easy". Introductory should mean "short and important". Information should be precise, with commentary that inspires its audience.
