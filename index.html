Hi I'm Luke. I'm an undergraduate maths student at Monash University. I really enjoy group theory, organic chemistry and piano. Recently I've been playing some very easy jazz pieces but I hope I'll have the time to return to form in the holidays. From either stubbornness or incompetence, I'm hopeless with lectures, so I have a lot of appreciation for clear books. In particular I'm grateful for Kenneth Kuttler's notes on analysis. The fields aside from maths are less fortunate in their undergrad books. "Introductory" should not mean "easy". Introductory should mean "short and important". Information should be precise, with commentary that inspires its audience.
